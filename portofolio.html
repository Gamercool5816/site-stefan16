Red Cross

 In Comanesti, Romania, at 16, I joined the Romanian Red Cross, driven by a deep sense of empathy. I underwent rigorous training in first aid, emergency response, and disaster preparedness. I spent my days teaching first aid classes and organizing community health workshops, sharing vital knowledge with my neighbors. In the city, I helped distribute food and medical supplies to those in need, experiencing firsthand the impact of our efforts. I also became an advocate, organizing fundraisers and teaching kids various activities. My dedication earned me several local awards, inspiring others to join the cause.

Hobbies

Ever since I can remember, I've been passionate about football. My love for the game started when I was a little kid, kicking a ball around in the backyard with my dad. Now, I play as a forward for my high school team. I live for the thrill of scoring goals and the camaraderie with my teammates. Every weekend, I watch professional matches, studying my favorite players' techniques. Football isn't just a sport to me; it's a way of life. Training hard, staying fit, and always striving to improve keeps me motivated. My dream is to play professionally one day, and I'm determined to make it happen.My favourite player is Cristiano Ronaldo and my favourite team is Real Madrid.

HTML

I'm constantly learning and keeping up with the latest trends in web design. My goal is to become a professional web developer and create stunning, user-friendly websites.
